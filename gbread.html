Garlic Bread Recipe
Servings - 4
Calories - 350
Prep Time - 20 min
Ingredients
  • 1/2 cup butter, melted
  • 3 to 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • loaf (1 pound) French bread, halved lengthwise
  • 2 tablespoons minced fresh parsley
Instructions
  1. In a small bowl, combine butter and garlic. Brush over cut sides of bread; sprinkle with parsley. Place, cut side up, on a baking sheet.
  2. Bake at 350Â° for 8 minutes. Broil 4-6 in. from the heat for 2 minutes or until golden brown. Serve warm.
